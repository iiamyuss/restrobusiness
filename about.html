Our Story

ScriptNoodles started with a simple idea: late-night coding sessions require high-quality fuel. We grew tired of standard instant ramen and decided to engineer the perfect bowl from scratch.


Located in the heart of Mumbai, we blend traditional Asian noodle techniques with a modern, fast-paced atmosphere. Whether you are debugging a complex application or just catching up with friends, our kitchen is compiling the best flavors just for you.
